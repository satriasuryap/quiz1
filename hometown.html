About Bekasi

Bekasi (Indonesian pronunciation: [ˈbəkasi]) is a city in West Java, Indonesia, located on the eastern border of Jakarta.
It serves as a commuter city within the Jakarta metropolitan area. According to the 2020 Census by Statistics Indonesia (BPS),
Bekasi had 2,543,676 inhabitants. The official estimate for mid 2021 was 2,564,940.[3] It lies within the largest metropolitan area in Indonesia (Jabodetabek).
The city is bordered by Bekasi Regency to the north and the east, Bogor Regency and Depok to the south, and East Jakarta to the west.
