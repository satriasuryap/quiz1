Pancong Balap

One of the must buy food when you come visit bekasi is Pancong Balap, as it is different from the regular pancong you will encounter in Indonesia.
Normally Pancong that you will encounter is a type of desert made of rice flour and coconut-based dough,
but in Bekasi it is made with a batter simmilar to kue cubit with a lot of modernized toppings such as, cheese, chocolatte, strawberry, peanut, blueberry, condensed milk, etc.
