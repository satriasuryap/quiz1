Summarecon Bekasi!

Bekasi is a metropolitan city, so there are actually not much tourist spot to go to, but if you happens to be in Bekasi try to visit Summarecon.
Summarecon is an area in Bekasi that has a shopping district, business district, and living district. As there are a lot of modern places for you to stay, shop, and start a business.
